Welcome to My HPT Portfolio

This website is meant to serve as a comprehensive resource for students, scholars, and professionals interested in exploring the field of Human Performance Technology. Through my academic journey and practical experience, I have developed a deep appreciation for the systematic approach that HPT provides in addressing performance gaps and enhancing organizational effectiveness.

I believe that HPT represents the future of performance improvement, combining scientific rigor with practical application to create meaningful change in individuals and organizations. My particular interest in technology in education has deepened my appreciation for how innovative tools and digital solutions can enhance learning outcomes and streamline performance interventions. My commitment to this field drives me to continuously learn, research, and share knowledge with the broader HPT community, especially exploring how emerging educational technologies can advance human performance in both academic and workplace settings.

What HPT Means to Me

Human Performance Technology represents more than just a professional discipline—it embodies a systematic, evidence-based approach to understanding and improving human performance in all its complexity. To me, HPT is about bridging the gap between theory and practice, using rigorous analysis to identify performance barriers and implementing interventions that create lasting, positive change.

The field resonates with me because it combines scientific methodology with human-centered design, recognizing that performance improvement isn't just about processes or technology—it's about people. HPT provides the framework to understand performance holistically, considering environmental, motivational, and systemic factors that influence human behavior and outcomes. This comprehensive approach ensures that interventions address root causes rather than symptoms, leading to sustainable performance improvements.

About This Website and Its Purpose

This portfolio website serves as a comprehensive resource for understanding Human Performance Technology and its applications. The site was developed as part of my academic coursework and professional development in HPT, designed to serve students, scholars, and professionals interested in exploring this dynamic field.

The website is organized into four main sections, each addressing key aspects of HPT knowledge and practice:

What is HPT

Provides foundational knowledge about Human Performance Technology, including comprehensive definitions, information about ISPI (International Society for Performance Improvement), professional standards, and a practical job aid for performance analysis processes.

HPT Models

Explores the theoretical frameworks that guide HPT practice, including detailed explanations of Gilbert's Behavior Engineering Model, Rummler-Brache Performance Model, and additional models, all described in original language with practical applications.

Case Analysis

Demonstrates practical applications of HPT through real-world case studies, showcasing systematic analysis processes, intervention design, and measurable results, along with personal reflections on the value of case-based learning.

Glossary

Serves as a comprehensive reference for essential HPT terminology, featuring 20 key concepts and definitions that form the foundation of professional HPT practice and communication.
